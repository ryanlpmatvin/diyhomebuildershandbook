Privacy Policy

DIY — Home Builders Handbook · Last updated: 28 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the DIY — Home Builders Handbook application ("the app", "we", "us") collects, uses, stores and protects your information. We comply with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020. By creating an account and using the app, you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account information — the name and email address you provide when you sign up.
  • Authentication data — a securely hashed password (we never store your password in plain text).
  • Usage data — which guides you mark as "learned", so we can show your progress.

We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, payment details, or any sensitive personal information. We do not use third-party advertising or analytics trackers.

How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account and let you sign in across devices.
  • To save and display your learning progress.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the service.

Where your information is stored

Account and progress data is stored using Supabase, a managed database and authentication provider, on secured cloud infrastructure. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS). Access is restricted so that you can only read and modify your own data.

Sharing your information

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information. We do not share it with third parties except our infrastructure provider (Supabase) strictly to operate the service, or where required by law.

Your rights

  • Access and correction — you can view and update your account details in the app.
  • Deletion — you can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Profile → Delete account within the app. This is immediate and irreversible.

Data retention

We retain your data only while your account is active. When you delete your account, your account record and all associated progress data are permanently removed.

Children

The app is intended for general home DIY use by adults. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Disclaimer

The app provides general guidance only and is not professional building advice. Building work may require council consent and licensed practitioners. See the in-app disclaimer for full details.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
